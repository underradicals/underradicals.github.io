Software Development

Software development is the structured process of designing, creating, testing, and maintaining software applications, systems, or platforms.

Project Management

Project management is the planning, organizing, executing, and controlling resources to achieve goals within a defined timeframe and budget.

Documentation

Documentation is the record of information, processes, decisions, and requirements that can be referred to by stakeholders in a SDLC.
